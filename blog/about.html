About this blog

Hi, welcome to my little website. Please take off your shoes and make yourself at home. Can I offer you something to drink?

Who I am

I'm Baptiste (IPA pronunciation: [ba'tist], pronouns: he/him), also known as @bmispelon in most online spaces.

I currently live in a quiet country town in Norway where I alternate between being a software developer and a linguistics student.

If you'd like to get in touch, you can write me on Mastodon, or email me at hello[at]bmispelon.rocks.

What I write about

The backlog on this blog isn't exactly huge right now, and my plans are still a little fuzzy on what exactly I'm going to write about.

I expect a lot of it will be about code, mostly Python and probably Django-related. But some of my other interests might also make appearences: I'm a big language nerd (both human and computer), and also an avid outdoor enthusiast (not hard when you live in Norway).

How this site is made

This site is made by hand, using plain HTML and a tiny custom blog engine written in Python.

Styling is done using pico.css.

The code is available on GitHub if you're interested.
